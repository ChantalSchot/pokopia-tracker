- Region cannot be changed after creation.
- House name must be unique within a region for that user; if you choose stronger uniqueness across all users in region, document it, but default should match the product requirement.
- Users have a set/list of registered Pokémon based on pokemon IDs.

- Changing a house region is not allowed after creation; if editing flow mentions region changes, implement as prohibited after create and explain this resolves contradictory wording safely.

  - HOUSING_KIT

  - CUSTOM = 4
  - HOUSING_KIT = based on housing kit size/capacity
- Items can be linked to many houses, and houses can have many items.
- Houses derive “present favourites” from assigned items.
- Suggested Pokémon for a house are based on active favourites and idealHabitat compatibility.
- If no items are present, suggestion fallback uses idealHabitat + favourites of Pokémon already in the house.
- If house is full, no suggestions should be shown.
- If a Pokémon assigned to a house has none of its favourites active in that house, show a warning state.

Important contradiction handling:
One requirement says “a pokemon can only be assigned to 1 house” while another says “pokémon can be added to (linked to) houses (one or multiple at the same time)”.

- a Pokémon may only be assigned to one house at a time
